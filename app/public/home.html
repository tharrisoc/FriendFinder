Friend Finder

This is a simple friend/date finder application demonstration.

It asks you to answer ten questions on a scale from one to five.

Once you respond to the questions and click the Submit Survey button, the application will match your responses against its database of existing members and will then display the name and photograph of your closest match.

[Note that since this is a demonstration, the questions in the survey are totally arbitrary; there was no method or intent used to select them.]
